### Pricing Psychology
- **Starter**: Loss-leader for solo entrepreneurs. Upgrade pressure: contact limit (500), 1 AI agent, no social automation.
- **Professional**: The "sweet spot" — small teams get everything they need. $99 is impulse price for businesses.
- **Agency**: High-margin plan. White-label justifies 3x price jump. Target: marketing/sales agencies reselling.
- **Annual discount**: 20% off (2 months free) — offered at signup and 30 days in.

## Customer Retention Strategy

### Onboarding (Week 1 = critical)
```
Day 0:  Welcome email (founder personal video)
Day 1:  "Set up SalesPal™ in 5 min" tutorial email
Day 3:  Milestone check: "Has your first AI conversation happened?"
Day 5:  Feature spotlight: Pipeline view
Day 7:  "Your first week summary" — OpsPal™ briefing
Day 14: NPS survey + personal call offer (for Pro+ users)
Day 30: Success story request + case study offer
```

### Expansion Revenue (NRR target: 115%)
- In-app usage notifications: "You've used 80% of your contact limit → Upgrade for unlimited"
- Monthly report email shows features NOT being used: "You haven't tried MarketingPal™ yet — here's what it can do"
- Upsell modal at natural moments (e.g., 490/500 contacts used)

### Churn Prevention
- At-risk detection: No login in 14 days → personal email from "Thabo" (founder)
- Downgrade trigger: Offer 50% off for 3 months before cancellation
- Exit survey → feed answers into product roadmap
- Win-back sequence: 30/60/90-day reengagement (free month if they return)
